Byunghwan Son

Global Affairs Program

George Mason University

Byunghwan “Ben” Son (손병환) is an Associate Professor of Global Affairs Program at George Mason University. He also serves as the director of the Asia-Pacific & Northeast Asian Studies minor program, the associate director of the Korean Studies Center, and a University Faculty Affiliate of Schar School of Policy & Government.
